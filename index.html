    Stock Average Calculator

    Calculate your average buy price across multiple purchases

    Enter values and click Calculate

    How it works

    When you buy the same stock multiple times at different prices, this calculator finds your average cost per share — helping you understand your breakeven point.

    FormulaAverage Price = Total Investment ÷ Total Quantity
    Tip: Buying more shares when price drops (averaging down) lowers your average cost and reduces the price needed to break even.

    SIP Calculator

    Systematic Investment Plan — grow wealth monthly

    Enter values and click Calculate

    How it works

    SIP lets you invest small amounts every month into mutual funds, leveraging the power of compounding over time.

    FormulaM = P × {[(1+r)^n – 1] / r} × (1+r)
    Tip: Starting early is the biggest advantage — even ₹1,000/month at 12% for 30 years becomes ₹35 Lakhs!

    EMI Calculator

    Calculate monthly loan installments instantly

    Enter values and click Calculate

    How it works

    EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is a fixed payment you make every month to repay a loan over a set period.

    FormulaEMI = P × r × (1+r)^n ÷ [(1+r)^n - 1]
    Tip: Even a 0.5% lower interest rate on a ₹50L loan can save you ₹1.5L+ over 20 years.

    CAGR Calculator

    Compound Annual Growth Rate — measure true investment growth

    Enter values and click Calculate

    How it works

    CAGR represents the mean annual growth rate of an investment over a period longer than one year — it smooths out volatility.

    FormulaCAGR = (Final Value ÷ Initial Value)^(1÷Years) − 1
    Tip: Compare CAGR of different investments to objectively pick the better performer.
